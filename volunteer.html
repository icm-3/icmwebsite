Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers support events, education, social service, prayer logistics, and community operations. Registered volunteers can share interests, track service hours, and request confirmation for completed service.

  • Event and program support
  • Education and youth support
  • Social welfare and food pantry service
  • Prayer logistics and community operations

Service Hours

  • Register before requesting service-hour certificates
  • Report event name, date, hours, and supervising committee lead
  • Keep confirmation for certificate requests
