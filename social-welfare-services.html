Social & Welfare Services

Community Care

ICM's Social and Welfare Services support the community through religious and social programs, charity, brotherhood, zakat and sadaqah distribution, food pantry support, financial aid, and family/community services.

  • Financial aid pathways
  • Family counseling and community care
  • Food pantry support
  • Other community services

Food Pantry

Normal hours: every first Saturday of the month, 9:00-11:00 AM.

Location: Islamic Center of Morrisville, 107 Quail Fields Ct, Morrisville, NC 27560.

Financial Aid

Requests may support rent, utility bills, basic needs, and disaster or calamity support depending on circumstances. Processing can take up to two weeks after documentation is received.
