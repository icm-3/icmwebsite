Nibraas Institute

Alim Bachelor's Degree Program

The Islamic Center of Morrisville is pleased to announce the launch of the Nibraas Institute, a leading educational institution committed to advancing Islamic higher education. The name "Nibraas," originating from the Arabic word "nibras", conveys the essence of enlightenment through knowledge as the Institute of Light. Nibraas Institute is dedicated to enhancing Muslim scholarship and guiding seekers on a path of deep wisdom. Beginning this Fall, the institute will offer a comprehensive four-year Alim Bachelor's Degree program in Islamic Studies.

Overview

  • Unveiling the secrets of the Arabic language, unlocking the Quran's profound beauty, and navigating classical Islamic texts with ease.
  • Embarking on a journey through Hadith sciences, unearthing the wisdom of the Prophet Muhammad's authentic sayings and practices.
  • Exploring diverse theological perspectives, enriching your understanding of Islam's rich intellectual tapestry.
  • Mastering the intricacies of Fiqh (jurisprudence), confidently navigating legal and ethical frameworks through renowned scholars' guidance.

Nibraas is more than an institute, it's a vibrant community. Learn from esteemed scholars, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and forge lasting bonds with fellow seekers of knowledge. Our supportive environment fosters personal growth and intellectual development, nurturing your passion for Islam.

Program Details

  • 4 year degree, with 8 total semesters
  • In person learning opportunities will be offered
  • Classes 3 times a week for 1 hour each
  • All courses taught by scholars, who have extensively studied their field

Graduates Will Be Prepared To

  • Serve as religious leaders and educators
  • Pursue further Islamic scholarship
  • Apply their knowledge in diverse professional settings
