Food Pantry

Food pantry support is handled in person at ICM during pantry operating hours.

Back to Services

Apply In Person

There is no online food pantry form. To apply for Food Pantry Services, please visit the Islamic Center of Morrisville during food pantry operating hours and fill out the application in person.

  • Normal hours: first Saturday of each month, 9:00-11:00 AM
  • Location: 107 Quail Fields Ct, Morrisville, NC 27560
  • Questions: socialwelfare@icmnc.org

Items Currently Accepted

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Cereal
  • Lentils
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Pasta and pasta sauce
