About ICM

Islamic Center of Morrisville

Serving Morrisville, West Cary, and the RTP area through worship, learning, service, and community connection.

Our Community

ICM was founded in Ramadan 2009 to serve the growing Muslim community around Morrisville and the greater RTP area. The masjid welcomes families and neighbors from many backgrounds and offers worship, education, youth activities, and service programs.

As the community has grown, ICM has continued to make room for multiple Jumu'ah shifts, family programming, youth engagement, and regular classes.

What ICM Offers

  • Daily prayers and Jumu'ah shifts
  • Quran and Islamic studies programs
  • Youth and family gatherings
  • Social and welfare services

Meet the Imam

Imam Manzar ul Islam al Azhari serves as Imam and resident scholar for the ICM community.

Imam Manzar ul Islam al Azhari

Imam Manzar studied traditional Islamic sciences before continuing Islamic studies at Al-Azhar University. His public biography notes further academic work in the United States and the United Kingdom, along with teaching and research across Islamic studies.

At ICM, he supports the community through classes, reminders, guidance, and scholarship.
