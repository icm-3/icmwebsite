Financial Aid Request

Confidential assistance requests are reviewed after the required documents are received. Processing can take up to two weeks.

Back to Services

Required Documents

Incomplete applications cannot be processed. Upload clear copies when submitting the request.

  • State ID or passport
  • Most recent federal income tax return
  • Last two pay stubs
  • Bank account statements for checking, savings, and investments
  • Bills, lease, contracts, or statements related to the aid request
