Quran Memorization & Reading Program

Al-Falah Quran School is a Quran memorization and reading program for Grade 1 and up. The program focuses on comprehensive Tajweed rules, personalized learning plans, and qualified and professional teachers.

  • Learn comprehensive Tajweed rules
  • Personalized learning plan
  • Qualified and professional teachers
  • $100/month one child with family discount
  • Convenient after school program
  • Monday - Thursday, 5:00 - 6:30 PM
  • Location: 107 Quail Fields Court, Morrisville NC 27560
