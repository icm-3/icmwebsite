Al Mizan Academy

Al Mizaan Leadership Academy provides Islamic education and leadership development for youth. ICM’s official page says the academy was created in 2011 through the Islamic Center of Morrisville to provide a close-knit weekend learning environment for kids in the local Muslim community.

The goal is to help students learn important Islamic teachings and Quranic Arabic while connecting that knowledge to daily life at home and school.

Who It Is For

Students from KG through high school.

Schedule

Sundays, 10:05 AM - 1:20 PM.

Main Features

  • Quranic Arabic reading, writing, and memorization
  • Aqeedah, Islamic studies, and Islamic history
  • Applied leadership skills for Muslim youth
  • Homework shared weekly through email

Contact & Links

Contact: almizaan@icmnc.org

Nibraas Institute

Nibraas Institute is ICM’s Islamic higher-education program. It offers a comprehensive four-year Alim Bachelor’s Degree program in Islamic Studies focused on Arabic, Quranic understanding, Hadith sciences, theology, and fiqh.

Who It Is For

Students seeking structured Islamic higher education and preparation for religious leadership, teaching, scholarship, or community service.

Program Structure

Four-year degree, eight semesters, in-person learning opportunities, and classes three times a week for one hour each.

Study Areas

  • Arabic language and classical Islamic texts
  • Quranic understanding and Hadith sciences
  • Theology and Islamic jurisprudence
  • Courses taught by scholars with field expertise

Contact & Links
