Education Programs

Nibraas Institute

A four-year Alim Bachelor's Degree program in Islamic Studies committed to advancing Islamic higher education.

  • Four-year degree with eight total semesters
  • In-person learning opportunities
  • Classes three times a week for one hour each
Open Program

Al Mizaan Leadership Academy

Inclusive Islamic education and leadership skills that empower youth to be future leaders in their homes and communities.

  • Weekend learning environment for local Muslim youth
  • Islamic teachings and Quranic Arabic
  • Leadership skills connected to daily life
Open Program

Al-Falah Quran School

Quran memorization and reading program with Tajweed, personalized learning plans, and qualified teachers.

  • For Grade 1 and up
  • Monday-Thursday, 5:00-6:30 PM
  • $100/month for one child with family discount
